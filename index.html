Who We Are?

Blinker is a cocktail bar that is dedicated to good times; good times for the patrons, good times for the staff and good times for the planet. We focus on making deliciously sustainable drinks that do not compromise flavour in our bid to make the world more sustainable. Our mission is to be a community orientated venue that does our bit for making the community a better place. We try to source the most local ingredients for our cocktails and for the kitchen (opening soon). We try to source all our beers from the local breweries and our spirits from local distilleries. We are conscious about our products being as sustainable as possible and even though some of our wines are shipped from the continent we try to ensure a small carbon footprint. We source local talent to set up our music events and offer a platform for emerging DJ’s and musicians through our “locally live” music night every Thursday.
